EEE FOR INNOVATION

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) is a popular engineering course that imparts knowledge related to electrical and machine technology, circuit analysis, telecommunication systems, power engineering and its related industries. A four-year undergraduate course, EEE also emphasises the manufacture and design of electrical devices and the integration of their components into complex systems
